Teams

Timesheets are generated based on teams and the hours entered by the timekeeper assigned to the team, are approved by a reponsible for that team

How pjtracker © works?



1. WBS definition

The WBS are imported from an interface. When the list of activities has been loaded, is responsibility of Project Control to determine if each activity can receive hours of management, direct or indirec type. This process is done only once because is very rare that an activity that is supposed to receive direct hours can change later on to receive only management hours. So the definition of the the type of hours is done at the beginning of the project only

2. Manpower Resources

The manpower will also be imported from an interface. The source of data can be an HR or payroll database that will be able to produce the list of workers currently active. This list is updated regularly thorouhg the same interface in order to include new hired staff or to remove those that have been terminated. After the import of the manpower the table of job titles (generated during this import process) shall be updated by identifying on each job title which type of hours the position will be able to book worked time. The types are direct, indirect and management hours

3. Equipment Resources

The equipment list will also be imported. The source is usually the assets table that exists in the Accounting or ERP sytem the company is using to support their businesss. The import of equipment from the interface is usually done only once at the beginning of the project. There is need to import the interface again only when there is new equipment being assigned to the project. Equipment assigned to the project that is not being used will not register hours

4. Teams Definition

A team is a working unit. It is compossed by staff and equipment. A team includes those workers and equipment that are usually assigned to activities that they must be together to perform. In a workshop there could be one or more teams involved in several activities. Per instance, a welding team. It will include a forerman or supervisor, some welders, grinders, helpers, etc. The staff working in HR and responsible for the payroll are also part of a team. The protocol staff are a team. The concept of team helps to centralize the input of data for timekeeping purposes. A team must have a timekeeper assigned and at least one approver for the timesheet. A team will be allowed to charge hours to specific project adctivities that are defined usually by the planner of the project. Inside this tool a team is work unit that is allowed to charge hours to predefined WBS only.

5. Timesheet Entry and Approval

The timekeeper assigned to input man and equipment hours for the team will do so in a daily basis. Once the timesheets has been completed the timekeeper will send the timesheet for review. This is done electronically and the review by the assiged user to review that team will be immediately notified. If there are more than one approver/reviewer assigned to a team once the first approval is done the workflow continues and notifies the next approver and so on until the timesheet is fully approved. As the team is allowed to charge hours to specific WBS assiged by the project, is not possible for the timekeeper to enter hours in activities that that team is suposed not to be involved. Once the timesheets are approved they are available for further consultation whenever is needed. The timesheet will save also the name and time of filling up by the timekeeper as well as the information of all the approvers that intervened.
