Personal Protection

BKS provide specially trained personnel to clients, for the provision of personal protection to such clients. We operate full and part-time teams involved in the protection of recognized leaders, corporate executives, dignitaries and celebrities. Our concerns include travel as well as family and residential protection. We can arrange for stockholders meetings, conference and convention protective services and can coordinate executive travel domestically and internationally in a safe and secure manner.

Escort Services

BKS provide escort services for discerning clients/customers and also ensure adequate monitoring and surveillance of the premises and movement of such clients. To be a Personal Protection Escort for a Client or Principal is a task of immense pride and responsibility. Our Escort Section Team members are Qualified and trained to be efficient, courteous, diligent and to act with integrity in all they do.

Maritime Services

In our bid to take full advantage of the Local Content Policy of the Federal Government of Nigeria, we have diversified into the marine sector by offering Offshore Security Services. We use our private security personnel in collaboration with Government Security Forces, The Nigerian Navy (NN), The Joint Task Force (JTF) and Mobile Police (MOPOL) to ensure that the maritime environment where assets are located are effectively and efficiently monitored and protected against insurgents.

About us

Beosun Korporate Services Limited (BKS) is one of Nigeria’s leading security mergency response company with office in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria.
Beosun Korporate Services Ltd is a well-known brand name in the onshore/offshore maritime, oil, gas, energy and industrial security sector in Nigeria. Established in 1988, Beosun Korporate Services Ltd has continued to offer quality and total security services to its numerous clients spread across the length and breath of the country.
Since Incorporation, our watchword has been “the expert in guard” and within a short time, the company has acquired the toga of leadership in the Nigerian security business.
Our very strong presence in every sector of the Nigerian economy is an eloquent testimony of our elaborate and efficient machinery geared towards the provision of distinguished security services to our clients at short notices, and we are forever growing.
Our Training programmes continually provide in-depth knowledge and skills to our operatives and staff, in turn contributing to the efficient services we are known for.

Mission

Vision

To become the premier outfit in Nigeria with Global Network achieved through commitment to excellence in the provision of qualitative security services.
